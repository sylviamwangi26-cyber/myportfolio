A showcase of my web development projects, software engineering solutions, and technical implementations.

Web Development & Software Engineering

MedPass

A comprehensive hospital management system architected to seamlessly connect multiple healthcare facilities, allowing patients instant, secure access to their complete medical histories from anywhere. Engineered with robust administrative oversight, the system assigns unique IDs to each hospital for real-time monitoring of staff metrics and incoming revenue streams. Crucially, it features uncompromising role-based access control (RBAC). For instance, accounting personnel are strictly restricted from specific database segments to drastically reduce the risk of internal embezzlement, requiring a specialized executive admin key to unlock sensitive financial intersections.

Firstplace Palace

An end-to-end real estate and property management platform tailored specifically for the dynamic Kenyan housing market. The platform serves a dual purpose: it empowers independent external sellers and agencies to register, list, and manage their own property portfolios through a dedicated seller dashboard, while simultaneously providing Firstplace Palace's internal team with advanced administrative tools to oversee, market, and seamlessly sell their proprietary properties. The system includes advanced search filtering and lead generation mechanics.

Salame Safaris

A visually immersive, performance-optimized digital platform developed for a premier tour and travel company launched earlier this year. The core mission of the project was to digitally encapsulate and broadcast the true, unadulterated authenticity of the Kenyan wilderness and cultural heritage to a global audience. The platform features dynamic booking workflows for exclusive wildlife excursions, curated travel packages, and local culinary experiences, utilizing high-resolution media galleries to inspire wanderlust and drive direct bookings.

Kioko Enterprise

A highly customized e-commerce and logistics solution built for a prominent local wines and spirits distributor operating in both bulk and retail sectors. The standout feature of this technical build is its sophisticated WhatsApp API integration, which serves as an automated, conversational delivery assistant. Clients can effortlessly place orders, specify precise alcohol types, and drop their location pins for destination routing directly within WhatsApp, with final checkout integrated via the Safaricom Daraja API for frictionless, secure M-Pesa mobile payments.

Marketing Data Analysis

An advanced analytical data visualization project born out of comprehensive marketing campaigns. By aggregating and synthesizing complex datasets from ad platforms, this tool provides deep, actionable insights into target audience demographics, behavioral patterns, and engagement metrics. This data-driven framework allows marketing teams to pivot strategies in real-time, optimizing ad spend and ensuring that highly tailored, relevant content reaches the precise user segments that need it most to ensure steady ROI tracking.
